# AfraMedico Intelligent Care Operating System (ICOS) — Product Roadmap

AfraMedico Intelligent Care Operating System (ICOS) evolved from AfraMedico Business Growth OS.

This roadmap organizes the product into staged platform maturity: foundation, operations, intelligence, and platformization.

## Phase 1: Foundation

Purpose: establish the core internal operating system and validate business workflows using frontend-only prototypes and local data.

Modules:

- Mission Control
- Authority CRM
- Referral Partner CRM
- Lead Management
- Case Workspace
- Referral Protection
- Clinical Decision Center
- Unified Patient Context

## Phase 2: Operations

Purpose: expand ICOS from growth and clinical coordination into full medical tourism operations.

Modules:

- Travel Operations Center
- Finance & Commission Center
- Hospital Relations Center
- Treatment Journey Center
- Follow-up Center
- Workflow & Access Control Center

## Phase 3: Intelligence

Purpose: add AI-assisted decision support, growth intelligence, attribution protection, and operational risk prediction while preserving human control.

Modules and capabilities:

- AI Medical Review
- OCR
- AI Clinical Summary
- AI Executive Assistant
- Attribution Protection Layer
- Growth Intelligence
- Risk Prediction

## Phase 4: Platformization

Purpose: convert the validated frontend operating model into a secure, auditable, multi-role platform.

Platform capabilities:

- Supabase backend
- Role-based access
- Audit-ready database
- Partner portal
- Patient portal
- Hospital portal
- API integrations

## Product Rule

Every future roadmap item must connect to stable patient, case, partner, hospital, referral, quote, task, and audit identifiers.

ICOS should not become a disconnected set of dashboards. It must remain one operating system.
