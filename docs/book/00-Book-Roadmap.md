# 00. Book Roadmap

## Purpose

This book grows naturally from the ICOS project.

It is written while ICOS is being built.

Nothing should be written only for publishing.

Everything should originate from real architectural decisions, real operational problems, real design corrections, and real discoveries made during the creation of the platform.

## Why This Book Exists

ICOS is not only a software product. It is the result of turning operational experience into structure.

The book should preserve that evolution.

It should capture:

- Why certain modules were created.
- Why the Case became the center of the system.
- Why organizational knowledge matters more than software features.
- Why human decision-making must remain central in healthcare, finance, and commission-sensitive workflows.
- Why the system grew from AfraMedico into a broader platform idea.

## How Chapters Should Grow

Each chapter should begin as a record of real work.

When a new module is created, a new business rule is discovered, or a workflow is corrected, the lesson should be captured here.

The manuscript should not pretend the platform appeared fully formed.

The path matters.

## Living Manuscript Rule

Append ideas.

Preserve chronology.

Do not erase earlier thinking simply because the architecture improves later.

The movement from uncertainty to clarity is part of the book.
